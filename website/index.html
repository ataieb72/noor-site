Noor — pray five times a day, consistently

Noor is a daily prayer-habit app for Android and iOS. It shows accurate prayer times for your city, gives you a one-tap checklist for the five daily prayers, and keeps a forgiving streak that encourages without shaming — plus a true Qibla compass, weekly insights, gentle reminders, and a clean Quran reader with recitation.

  • No account. No ads. Your data stays on your device.
  • Prayer times computed on-device — works offline.
  • Quran content provided by the Quran.Foundation Content API.
